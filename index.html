Fitness First Fusionopolis


Located on the 23rd – 25th stories of the iconic and distinctive Fusionopolis, members indulge in floor-to-ceiling panoramic 360-degree views while working out. This deluxe 18,632 square feet club boasts 3 levels of floor space with state-of-the-art, world-class equipment.

Get a bit of the outdoors by taking a relaxing dive in the 25-metre, 5-lane roof-top swimming pool. Or head indoors to the large Group Exercise and beautiful Mind and Body studios and work up a sweat in a vast array of classes. Post-workout, recharge and unwind at our Relaxation Zone comprising of 2 beautiful sky gardens, sauna, as well as our members’ lounge.

Access to the club is convenient, with the One North MRT station at the basement of the building. Fitness First Fusionopolis is conveniently located near the Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE), Holland Village MRT and Buona Vista MRT. A shuttle bus service to Fusionopolis is available from Buona Vista MRT.
