CLASSES

Prefer to workout in a group? Come join our classes!

AERIAL FLOW YOGA

Elevate your practice with Aerial Yoga. The entire class takes place mid-air as you flow from pose to pose on a Yoga Swing. This unique prop takes the strain off your body letting your practice be longer and more comfortable.

Your core will work hard to support your back. But there is relief to be had too. Feel its restorative and therapeutic benefits as the inversions alleviate the tightness in your neck, shoulder and lower back.



H20 HIIT

Take your High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workout to the pool. Performed on a floating aqua mat, you will challenge and improve your strength and stability with 30 minutes of cardio and resistance type movements. It is a demanding workout that will push you to your limits!



PRO 45

At Pro Cycling, every class we ride using different tracklist towards the goal. You’ll race through pre choreographed and timed interval drills that all work towards that specific training goal. It could be speed, endurance, or lower body strength. Every 3 months, we’ll launch a new version with new tracklist always adding on to our library change up the training stimulus. Come Fight through sprints, climbs or longer drills!
